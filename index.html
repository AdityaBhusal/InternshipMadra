NEPAL DIGITAL ECONOMY SUMMIT-2024

September 28th - 29th
Hotel Soaltee Crowne Plaza, Kathmandu, Nepal

About the Conference

The Nepal Digital Economy Summit 2024, organized by the Nepal Digital Association (NDA), Innovative Circle, and MaDRA, is a pioneering event aimed at leveraging the immense socio-economic potentials of the digital revolution in Nepal. The summit will bring together governments, businesses, development partners, academia, and communities to discuss and enhance their capacities in the digital economy.


Key themes include health and education (SDG-3 and 4), tourism, environment and climate change (SDG-13), digital public infrastructure, agriculture (SDG-2), financial institutions, financing and investment, legal and regulatory frameworks, and the energy sector (SDG-7 and 9). Discussions will focus on utilizing disruptive technologies like AI, Blockchain, IoT, Cybersecurity, and Big Data to develop e-commerce measures, policies, and legal frameworks.

This summit aims to foster meaningful collaboration and set milestones for Nepal's digitization process, addressing the Digital SDGs set by the National Planning Commission.

The 9th International Conference on Digital Economy (ICDEc) will be held at the Faculty of Juridical, Economic and Social Sciences (FSJES - Souissi), Mohammed V University, Rabat, Morocco from May 9th to 11th, 2024. The ICDEc conference is an opportunity to bring together researchers and experts in emerging technologies from both public and private sectors to discuss issues related to the development, implementation, use and management of emerging technologies and the move to digital economy. The conference is an occasion to link researchers from computer science, business computing and information system areas to business fields such as marketing, finance, human resources, management etc. It is an opportunity to explore ideas, exchange thoughts and implement research projects between this diversity of fields with a focus on application contexts like e-commerce, e-business, e-heath, e-learning, etc.
